# 📘 Surviving the Shift: Managing Campus-to-Corporate Transition Shock

## 📖 Overview

This repository contains my academic research project, **"Surviving the Shift: Managing Campus-to-Corporate Transition Shock,"** completed as part of the **Business Etiquette and Personality Development** course during my Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) program.

The research explores the challenges fresh graduates encounter when transitioning from academic life to the corporate workplace. It examines the concept of **transition shock** and discusses the skills, mindset, and strategies required to adapt successfully to professional environments.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Understand the concept of campus-to-corporate transition shock.
* Identify the major challenges faced by fresh graduates.
* Explore the role of emotional intelligence, adaptability, and professional behavior.
* Analyze established theories and frameworks related to workplace readiness.
* Provide practical recommendations for a successful transition into corporate life.
